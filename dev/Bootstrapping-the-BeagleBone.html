Bootstrapping the BeagleBone

Installation is different for the BeagleBone Black and the original BeagleBone. The steward is currently being tested on both these platforms, and partial installation instructions for the two different platforms exist but aren't guaranteed to work, or even be complete.

The steward is also mostly functional, but without mdns capability on the following platform,

  • BeagleBone Black

Instructions for the following platform are currently incomplete. Please see the instructions for the Raspberry Pi and the BeagleBone Black for hints to how to carry through to completion,

  • BeagleBone Rev.A6 (white)
